About me


Hi! I am Leonel Contrano.


The question of why I chose to study Engineering is often asked, but the answer doesn't stem from a specific moment of realization. In fact, pursuing Engineering has been a lifelong aspiration for me. The desire to create and design spaces has been ingrained in me from a young age, and I've always known that this was the field I wanted to pursue. So, when asked why I studied Engineering, the answer is simple: it's been my lifelong pursuit.

Over the years, my experience in the field of Engineering has allowed me to witness the vast diversity of this profession. It's been truly awe-inspiring to see how engineering can have such a significant impact on the culture and history of the places it inhabits. This realization has instilled in me a great sense of pride and responsibility as an Engineer. To continue to grow and excel in my chosen profession, I am actively seeking out opportunities to learn from experienced mentors and develop my skills as a new Engineer.

Today, I am a Freelancer as a designer, illustrator, and artist. I take every bit of inspiration from my childhood into my everyday work.

I don't just design spaces - I tell stories through them.
